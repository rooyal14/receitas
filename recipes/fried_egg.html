Ovo frito

Descriptione

This is probably the fastest and simplest recipe of the list. The fried egg is a good choice when there's nothing to eat and few to feed.

Ingredients

  • Egg
  • Rice
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Break the egg and put it in a pan(without the eggshell)
  2. Fry the egg until it is in a consistency that you like.
  3. Cook the rice(don't forget to put salt in it)
  4. Cool and serve
