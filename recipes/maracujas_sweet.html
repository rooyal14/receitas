Doce de maracujá

Description

This is my best creation. As it's own name says, it is a sweet made of maracujá. It is not recommended to anyone that have some type of lactose intolerance, since there goes a lot of milk derivatives.

Ingredients

  • Maracuja pulp
  • Condensed milk
  • Milk cream

Steps

  1. Put the maracuja pulp in the fridge
  2. Put the milk cream in the fridge
  3. Cut open the maracuja pulp
  4. Cut open the condensed milk
  5. Cut open the milk cream
  6. Throw everything in the blender
  7. Wait
  8. Throw everything everything in some recipient that you like
  9. Eat it
